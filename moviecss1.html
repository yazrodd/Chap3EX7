The Matrix

Everyday life is turned upside-down when Thomas Anderson discovers that the world around him is a detailed virtual reality created by a computer that has taken over the Earth of the future. When Anderson realizes of his predicament he teams up with Morpheus, the leader of a gang of freedom fighters, to reclaim their individuality and 'wake up' the world.
